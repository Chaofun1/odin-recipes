Curry Udon

Description

Today's recipe will be be Japanese Curry Udon. We will be using premade curry blocks that you can obtain from any Asian Supermarket and make some delicious Curry Udon within 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 servings Frozen Udon
    •
  • 2 cubes Curry Roux
    •
  • 1 onion
  • 6 oz of Sliced Beef
  • 2 tsp Soy Sauce
    •
  • 1 tbsp neutral-flavored oil
  • 3 cups dashi
    •
  • 1 tbsp sake
    •
  • 2 green onions/scallions

Instructions

  1. Gather all the ingredients. Break the curry roux into cubes and use 2 pieces. (keep the leftovers in an airtight container and store in the refigerator/freezer for 1-3 months). Prepare the dashi by boiling 3 cups of water.
  2. After the water finished boiling, put in 3 tbsp of dashi powder and place it aside.
  3. Thinly slice the onion and green onions. Set aside the green onions for garnish.
  4. Preheat a medium pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. When the pot is hot, add the oil. Next add the onion slices.
  5. Saut√© the onion slices for 2-3 minutes. Then, add the meat.
  6. Cook the meat until it's barely pink. Next, add the sake.
  7. Add the dashi to the pot and cover with the lid. Reduce the heat to medium low and cook for 5 minutes.
  8. When simmering, skim off the scum and fat from the stock with a fine-mesh skimmer and continue to cook.
  9. Meanwhile, start boiling a large pot of water for the udon.
  10. After 5 minutes of simmering the stock and meat, turn off the heat. Next, put a cube of curry roux in a ladleful of hot stock.
  11. With chopsticks or a spoon, dissolve the roux cube completely in the ladle, then release it to the soup. Repeat with the next cube. Tip: You don't want to eat a chunk of undissolved curry roux, so take your time to dissolve it completely.
  12. Add the soy sauce and mix well. Turn off the heat and cover with the lid to keep it warm.
  13. When the water is boiling, cook your udon noodles according to the package instructions (for this Sanuki udon, I heated the frozen noodles in boiling water for 1 minute).
  14. Drain the noodles and divide into two bowls. Pour the curry soup over the udon noodles. Top with the green onions and serve immediately.
